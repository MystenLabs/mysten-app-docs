# Sui Wallet by Mysten Labs

Use the [Sui Wallet browser extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sui-wallet/opcgpfmipidbgpenhmajoajpbobppdil) to manage your digital assets on the Sui network. Sui Wallet displays the coins and NFTs in your account, and lets you easily acquire new ones and transfer ones you own. You can also use Sui Wallet to stake your SUI and earn rewards. Connect Sui Wallet to dApps running on Sui to perform transactions.
